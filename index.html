About Us

Located just south of the U.S/ Candian border on 50 beautiful Pacific Northwest acreage, we are a year-round equestrian facility that can support all your equestrian needs. From complete novice to a grandprix contenders, we have the facilities to help you and your horse reach the top of your potential.

We host a variety of tournaments, schools, and shows throughout the year as well as partnering with local and international instructors to provide individual lessons and group clinics.

Services

Lessons Boarding Breeding Shows

Lessons

We work with Local Independent Instructors to provide you and your horse with the best possible learning environment. We have four indoor and four outdoor arenas that can be setup for almost any equestrian discipline. Don't have a horse, not a problem. We have a 24 stall barn of horses that are available for lessons and leasing.

Boarding

Have a horse, or multiple, and want to provide them with daily exercise, premium hay, and plenty of water. We have a separate 24 stall barn specifically for year-round boarding. Is your horse the "Ruged outdoor" type, we also pasture boarding options so your horse can run and be one of the herd.

Breeding

We retain a veterinarian, on call 24/7 to handle any horse related concerns. During the Breeding season, we also convert one of our smaller stables to mare/foul care. Helping to provide your mare with a minimal stress environment.

Shows

Thanks to our multiple arenas, we are able to host shows year-round. Our busiest season in the Pacific Northwest is Late Spring to Early Fall as we like to make use of endless days of sunshine before the rain returns.
